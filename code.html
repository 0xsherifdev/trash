Our Heritage

A Legacy of Judicial Distinction

Unurhoro & Co is a premier full-service law firm deeply rooted in the foundational excellence of the late Honourable Justice (Chief) Mitaire Unurhoro.

Called to the English Bar in 1961, Justice Unurhoro returned to Nigeria where his eloquence and sharp legal mind earned him the distinction of "The Canary of the Bar." This legacy of intellectual rigor and ethical brilliance continues to guide our firm today.

From his distinguished career at the Bar to his elevated service on the Bench, Justice Unurhoro's life was a testament to the pursuit of justice. At Unurhoro & Co, we carry this torch, providing premium legal solutions that honor our history while addressing modern legal complexities.

Leadership

Genevieve Unurhoro

Founder & Principal Counsel | LL.B, BL

Genevieve Unurhoro is an accomplished legal practitioner with an illustrious career spanning several decades. Before founding Unurhoro & Co, she honed her expertise at the prestigious firm of Thompson Okpoko, SAN & Partners, where she handled complex litigation and corporate matters at the highest levels.

She is a certified professional with the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO), bringing specialized knowledge in global IP protection. Furthermore, her dedication to excellence in corporate governance is evidenced by her membership in the Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators of Nigeria (ICSAN).

Her leadership is defined by the same "integrity first" philosophy that characterized the career of the late Justice Mitaire Unurhoro, ensuring that every client receives meticulous, results-oriented representation.
